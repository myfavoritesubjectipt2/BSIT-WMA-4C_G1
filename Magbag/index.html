Hi, I’m Jeremiah, born on October 9, 2001, and residing in Guevara, La Paz, Tarlac. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Tarlac State University, focusing on Web and Mobile Applications. My goal is to excel in my studies and graduate, equipping myself with the skills needed for a successful career in technology. I am passionate about innovation and excited to contribute my knowledge and creativity to future projects.

ELEMENTARY

I graduated from Guevara Elementary School in 2014, a place where I spent some of my most formative years. Transferring there in grade 4, I quickly embraced the vibrant community and supportive environment. My time at Guevara was filled with joyful memories, from engaging classroom lessons to participating in school events. The teachers nurtured my curiosity, fostering a love for learning that laid the foundation for my future academic pursuits. Reflecting on those years, I am grateful for the friendships I made and the valuable lessons I learned, all of which shaped who I am today.

HIGH SCHOOL

I graduated from Guevara High School in 2017, a pivotal time in my life where I truly discovered my passions and potential. Continuing my journey from elementary school, Guevara High provided a supportive environment that encouraged exploration and growth. Throughout my high school years, I engaged in various activities, from academic clubs to sports, which helped me build lasting friendships and develop essential life skills. The dedicated teachers inspired me to challenge myself and strive for excellence, instilling a strong work ethic that I carry with me today. Looking back, my time at Guevara High was filled with invaluable experiences that prepared me for the next chapter of my education.

SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

After completing high school, I took a significant step in my academic journey by enrolling at STI College Tarlac to pursue my passion for computers. Studying Mobile App and Web Development (MAWD) over the course of two years allowed me to delve deeper into the world of technology. My curiosity for computers blossomed as I engaged with hands-on projects and learned the intricacies of coding, design, and application development. The supportive environment at STI College fueled my enthusiasm, encouraging me to explore innovative ideas and develop practical skills. This experience not only solidified my technical foundation but also prepared me for the exciting challenges ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

COLLEGE

Currently, I am in my fourth year at Tarlac State University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a focus on Web and Mobile Applications. This program has allowed me to build upon the skills and knowledge I gained during my time at STI College Tarlac. My journey in college has been enriching, filled with challenging coursework and hands-on projects that further ignite my passion for technology. I appreciate the collaborative environment at Tarlac State University, where I can engage with fellow students and experienced faculty, all while deepening my understanding of web and mobile development. As I approach graduation, I am excited to apply what I’ve learned and contribute to the dynamic field of IT.
